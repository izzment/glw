Zumunta'n Mata
Coming together to help end period poverty

What's The Situation?

Sustainability

On average a woman menstruates for about 7 years during her lifetime. Reusable options help to manage that time in a way that is beneficial to their bodies but also to the earth.

Location

In low-income communities, many women struggle to afford sanitary products. Improving access to menstrual products in low-income communities is a pressing challenges

Menarche

96% of girls in Nigeria lack period education prior to menarche. Period parties create a space to talk about menstruation and prepare girls to handle their first period with confidence.

Menstrual Hygiene

The use of cloth strips or foams can cause health issues including urinary tract infections caused by poor hygiene. Education about best practices helps mitigate these issues.
